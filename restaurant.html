Our Story

Crafted in
open flame

We believe fire is the oldest, most honest form of cooking. Chef Marco Vidal built Ember & Ash around a single wood-burning hearth — no gas, no shortcuts. Just timber sourced from local orchards, ingredients from farms we know by name, and technique honed over two decades across three continents.

The Experience

More than a
meal

I

The Hearth Bar

Sit steps from our open wood-burning hearth. Watch the flames, smell the smoke, sip a glass of aged Malbec while your course is still on the fire.

II

Chef's Table

A private six-seat table inside the kitchen. A ten-course journey through the evening's harvest, plated and narrated by Chef Vidal himself.

III

Private Events

The full dining room is yours — birthdays, proposals, corporate evenings. We curate every detail from the first ember to the final glass.
